# Human Decision Card

## Trigger

Load this card when the milestone enters `AWAITING_HUMAN` or a mandatory decision trigger fires.

## Required action

- pause phase progression
- summarize the trigger and why it blocks progress
- present options with risks
- record a human decision artifact

## Allowed decision values

- `approve`
- `reject`
- `modify`
- `defer`

## Minimum contents

- milestone and phase
- trigger id or reason
- blocking issue summary
- options considered
- recommended option with rationale
- evidence references
- final human decision
